Core Features

Everything You Need to Stay Safe

AI-powered protection that works around the clock, even when you can't respond manually.

One-Tap Emergency SOS

Instantly alert your trusted contacts with your live GPS location. A single tap sends SMS, calls, and push notifications — even if your screen is locked.

Live GPS Tracking

Continuous location sharing with your safety circle. Your trusted contacts can track your journey in real-time on a secure map.

Silent AI Guardian

Detects distress keywords like "help" or "stop" and analyzes voice stress patterns to automatically trigger emergency alerts — no manual action needed.

Fake Call & AI Warning

Trigger a realistic fake incoming call to escape uncomfortable situations. The AI voice can also warn potential attackers that authorities are being notified.

Auto Audio Recording

Automatically begins recording audio during emergencies, creating evidence that is securely encrypted and cloud-stored for later use by authorities.

Crowd Risk Intelligence

Community-powered safety: when multiple users report danger in an area, it's automatically marked as unsafe on the map for everyone nearby.
